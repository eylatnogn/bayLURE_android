# bayLURE Privacy Policy

_Effective date: [SET DATE WHEN PUBLISHING]_

bayLURE is a fishing-conditions app. It has **no accounts, no ads, no
analytics, and no tracking**. We (the developer) run no servers and receive
none of your data.

## Data stored on your device only

Your catch log (species, gear, notes, sizes), attached photos, saved favorite
spots, presets, and settings are stored **only on your device**. They are
never uploaded anywhere. Deleting the app deletes them.

## Location

When you use your location (or type an address / drop a pin), the app sends
those **coordinates** to third-party weather and marine data services to fetch
conditions for that spot:

- **Open-Meteo** — weather, marine and hourly forecast data
- **NOAA (CO-OPS / Chart Display Service)** — tide predictions and nautical
  chart overlays
- **OpenStreetMap (Nominatim + map tiles)** — address search and the map
- **Open-Topo-Data (GEBCO)** — water depth estimates
- **iNaturalist** — fish species reported near your area
- **unpkg.com CDN** — loads the map display library

Coordinates are used transiently to answer each request. We do not store
them, and they are not linked to your identity. Each service processes
requests under its own privacy policy. Location access is optional — the app
works with a typed address or a dropped pin, and you can deny the location
permission at any time in system settings.

## Photos

The photo library permission is used only when you choose to attach a photo
to a catch. The photo is copied, resized, and kept on your device. It is
never uploaded.

## Children

bayLURE is not directed at children under 13 and collects no personal
information from anyone.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a
