## Full description (both stores)

```
Read the conditions. Tie on the right thing.

bayLURE turns live conditions at your fishing spot into a bite forecast and a
ranked list of lures, rigs, and bait — with the reason behind every pick.

A BITE SCORE THAT KNOWS YOUR FISH
• Tuned to your target species — trout, bass, redfish, and catfish don't
  feed at the same temperatures, and your score knows the difference
• Seasonal patterns built in: pre-spawn, spawn, and the fall feed-up
• Moon phase and solunar feeding windows, graded hour by hour with the best
  bite times highlighted
• LEARNS FROM YOUR CATCHES: every fish you log tunes your forecast toward
  the conditions you actually catch fish in
• Rivers too — live USGS streamflow, because rising or falling water can
  make or break a river bite

LIVE CONDITIONS, HONESTLY SOURCED
• Barometric pressure and its trend — the biggest bite factor
• Wind, clouds, humidity, sunrise/sunset, rain chance — with a lightning
  warning when thunderstorms are in the forecast
• Water temperature, NOAA tide predictions, and current tide movement
• Animated weather radar with a 2-hour storm forecast, an animated wind
  overlay, NOAA nautical charts, and depth contours right on the map
• Straight with you: if a data source is down, bayLURE says so instead of
  guessing quietly

THE PLANNER
• Tides & Bite: an hourly chart of the tide curve over your bite score —
  peak windows highlighted, tap any hour to see that moment's conditions
• Bite score 0–100 for today or any of the next 6 days
• Ranked lures, rigs, and bait matched to conditions, structure, clarity,
  species, and fishing pressure — each with rod, line, and hook advice
• Inshore, freshwater, AND offshore: mahi, grouper, tuna, king mackerel,
  cobia, snapper, amberjack, wahoo — with where they hold in the column
• Playbooks for tough, pressured water, expected fish nearby, and a link
  to your state's official fishing regulations

YOUR MAP, YOUR WAY
• Satellite imagery to read grass lines, channels, and structure (topo
  toggle included), two-finger rotation, and one-tap re-centering
• The map remembers your layer choices — open the app, it's set up your way
• GPS, address/ZIP search, or drop a pin; save favorite spots and bayLURE
  reopens on the last one you viewed

CATCH LOG THAT PAYS YOU BACK
• Log species, gear, size, notes, and a photo — conditions attach
  automatically, and you can backdate catches
• YOUR PATTERNS: see the conditions you really catch fish in, and get
  flagged when an upcoming day matches
• Back up spots, presets, and catches to a file you keep
• Everything stays on your device: no account, no sign-up

Fish smarter mornings. bayLURE is free, supported by a single small ad.
```
