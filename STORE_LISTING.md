## Full description (both stores)

```
Read the conditions. Tie on the right thing.

bayLURE turns live conditions at your fishing spot into a bite forecast and a
ranked list of lures, rigs, and bait — with the reason behind every pick.

SET YOUR SPOT
• Use GPS, type an address or ZIP, or drop a pin on the map
• Save favorite spots with your own labels and reload them in a tap
• Freshwater or saltwater, with structure and cover options to match

LIVE CONDITIONS
• Barometric pressure and its 3-hour trend — the biggest bite factor
• Wind, clouds, humidity, sunrise/sunset, and moon phase
• Water temperature (measured offshore, estimated for freshwater)
• NOAA tide predictions and current tide movement (saltwater)
• Charted water depth at your spot, plus an animated wind overlay and
  NOAA chart depths right on the map

A PLAN, NOT JUST A NUMBER
• Bite score 0–100 for today or any of the next 6 days, graded hour by hour
  with the best feeding windows highlighted
• Ranked lures, rigs, and bait matched to conditions, structure, clarity,
  target species, and fishing pressure — each with rod, line, and hook advice
• A read on what the fish are doing and playbooks for tough, pressured water
• Expected fish nearby, from real reported sightings, and a link to your
  state's official fishing regulations

CATCH LOG
• Log species, gear, size, notes, and a photo
• Conditions are attached to each catch automatically — build your own
  database of what works
• Everything stays on your device: no account, no ads, no tracking

Fish smarter mornings. bayLURE is free.
```
